PROBLEM

The size, weight, and energy consumption of hardware capable of supporting smart robots - those with capabilities similar to humans - prevent the robots of our future from being realized.
This obstacle can be overcome with cloud robots that share a ‘brain’ via cloud connectivity.

Existing policy organizations are not focused on cloud robots, lack the necessary expertise, or are too slow
Accordingly, a gap exists between tech advances and policy, and the gap is widening!
Because people are wary of robots and big tech companies having a presence in their personal lives, new platforms and approaches are needed that are worthy of trust.
The best minds are needed to represent stakeholders in shaping standards, best practices and other policies for robots we can live with.

SOLUTION

Collaboration

The Acro stakeholder community serves as a locus for information and discussion on cloud robotics policy concerns and solutions. Dialogue occurs via a virtual platform and in-person conference events.

Standardization

Acro policy solutions encompass the development of standards, templates for agreements, principles, and voluntary best practices. Acro certifications provide criteria and processes for AI engines, cloud operators, robots, and other interfaces to be evaluated and certified for interoperability, safety, reliability or security.

Accountability

Acro’s Cloud Robot Safety Board is an expert panel that investigates the probable cause of accidents associated with cloud robots in order to promote safety going forward from lessons learned.

STAKEHOLDERS

  • People (and other end users) will have higher quality (safer and consistent) and more intelligent robots at a lower cost

  • Robotics Companies will be able to offer smarter robots that are more affordable with easy access to an ever growing number of AI engines via cloud connectivity with interoperability, safety, reliability and security.

  • AI Developers (and other suppliers) will have channels to distribute their products and services easily to a growing number of robots and smart machines

  • Stakeholders and Governments will benefit from the checks and balances for the public benefit created by the fast emerging industry
